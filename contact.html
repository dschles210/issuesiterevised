I may not be a mental health professional, but I am here to listen to your story if you need it. I offer my support, love, and what resources I have found for those struggling with mental illness and feel a lack of support around you. I also offer a list of links for starting services for those that do not know where to start when seeking help.

E-mail: TAMHhelps@gmail.com

Link to Suicide Hotline: Visit the suicide hotline if in need.

Link to Eating Disorder Recovery: Visit the eating disorder recovery if you are in need of information on your disorder/s and want to start your recovery process.

Link to National Helpline for Mental Health and Substance Abuse: Visit SAMHSA to gain information on mental health, substance abuse, and are in need of guidance.

Link to Depression and Bipolar Support: Visit SAMHSA for more information on depression and bipolar disorders.

Link to Depression and Anxiety Support: Visit ADAA for more information on depression and anxiety disorders

Link to Domestic Violence Support: Visit the hotline if in need of support from domestic violence.

Link to Anti-Sexual Violence Support: Visit RAINN if in need of information or support from sexual violence.
